# Affiliate Case Studies

Real-world affiliate marketing examples with verified metrics.

## Case Study 1: LinkedIn — HeyGen Affiliate

**Platform:** LinkedIn
**Product:** HeyGen (AI video generation)
**Strategy:** "I tested X" stories posted 2-3 times weekly
**Results:** ~$2,400/month
**Conversion Rate:** 2.3% click-to-trial

**Key Finding:** "Posts showing before/after video results got 5x more engagement than feature lists."

**Tactics:**
- Personal storytelling format ("I tested HeyGen for 30 days...")
- Before/after visual comparisons
- Authentic tone — sharing real results, including limitations
- Link in first comment (LinkedIn algorithm optimization)

## Case Study 2: Blog SEO — Semrush Affiliate

**Platform:** Blog / Organic Search
**Product:** Semrush (SEO tool suite)
**Strategy:** Five targeted articles optimized for buyer-intent keywords
**Results:** ~$3,000/month
**Conversion Rate:** Organic search traffic to affiliate click

**Key Finding:** "Comparison posts ('Semrush vs Ahrefs') convert 3x better than standalone reviews."

**Tactics:**
- Hub-and-spoke content architecture
- Targeted "X vs Y" comparison keywords
- Detailed feature-by-feature breakdowns with screenshots
- Updated quarterly to maintain rankings

## Case Study 3: Twitter/X — Webflow Affiliate

**Platform:** Twitter/X
**Product:** Webflow (no-code website builder)
**Strategy:** Tutorial-focused threads
**Results:** ~$1,800/month recurring commission
**Conversion Rate:** Thread engagement to signup

**Key Finding:** "Tutorial threads outperformed opinion threads 4:1."

**Tactics:**
- Step-by-step tutorial threads (5-10 tweets)
- Visual demonstrations with screenshots/GIFs
- "How I built X with Webflow" narrative format
- Link in final tweet or reply

## Key Benchmarks

- **Realistic timeline to first commission:** 3-6 months
- **Typical conversion rates:** 1-5% (click to action)
- **Content lifespan:** Blog posts (years), social posts (days-weeks), videos (months)
- **Platform-appropriate content types matter more than volume**
